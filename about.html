WHAT IS MAKEUP?

Makeup is used to either subtly enhance features or, at other time, heavier and more defined makeup is used for special events. Cleopatra is known to have used pomegranate seeds to make lipstick while ancient Sumerians have used crushed gemstones to decorate their faces.The technique used during the application of makeup makes a lot of difference in how your face appears and this is where most people falter. Makeup is an art not many people take seriously. There are certain things one should know about their makeup.
